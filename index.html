Our Commitment

The 100+% Pledge

Kliesaud Industries is proud to be a founding signatory of the 100+% Pledge — a commitment to donate more than 100% of our annual revenue to charitable causes. Through a combination of leveraged giving, forward-dated pledges, matching multipliers, and frankly some accounting we don't fully understand ourselves, we have somehow managed to give away more money than we make. Every year. Our CFO has stopped asking questions. Our auditors have started asking a lot of them. We remain committed.
